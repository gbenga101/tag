WHITEPAPER

TAG is building the identity layer for Web3 — where your name, your vibe, your story becomes your signature. It's not just about owning a wallet address or a static NFT — it’s about owning how you express yourself. With TAG, users can mint their own customizable identity — names, moods, or creative aliases — and interact socially through on-chain tagging. The platform merges expression, recognition, and creativity into a dynamic ecosystem powered by NFTs and the $TAG token. TAG makes digital identity fluid, expressive, and social — and it’s designed to evolve with you.
